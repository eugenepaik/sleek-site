Getting Here
Where is the entrance?

Sleek is inside Koreatown Plaza at 928 S Western Ave. Take the main plaza entrance and head to Suite 333.

Is there parking?

Yes — free validated parking inside Koreatown Plaza, with plenty of space. Parking is included with your membership.

What’s the closest Metro station?

Wilshire / Western on the Metro D Line is a short walk north. Koreatown is one of the densest, most walkable neighborhoods in LA — most members will arrive on foot.

Can I tour the space?

Yes — stop by during opening hours, or reach out to schedule a guided tour. We’re happy to walk you through the floor, the pilates studio, and answer any questions.

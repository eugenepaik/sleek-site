The Idea

One standard, and it’s the best one.

Most gyms are built on trade-offs. Cheaper plates. Crowded floors. Machines that were almost the right choice. Sleek starts from the other direction — the best equipment we can source, laid out with the room to actually use it, in a space that rewards people who take their training seriously.

Koreatown has needed this for a long time. We’re building it.

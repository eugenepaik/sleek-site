What’s Included

Everything you need. Nothing you don’t.

Every open-gym membership includes unlimited access during operating hours, showers and changing rooms, towel service, and full use of the strength and cardio floors. No locker fees. No guest fees baked into surprise bills. Classes and one-on-one sessions are priced separately, on their own, so you only pay for what you use.

FAQ
Where is Sleek?

Sleek is inside Koreatown Plaza at 928 S Western Ave, Suite 333, Los Angeles. Open 7am to 11pm, seven days a week. Plan a visit.

Is there a contract or commitment?

Both options are available. Month-to-month is fully flexible — cancel anytime. A fixed-term contract brings your monthly rate down in exchange for committing to a set length. Reach out for current contract options.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes — freezes are available for travel, injury, or any reason. See your Membership Agreement for current freeze terms.

Do you offer day passes or guest passes?

Yes — day passes are $35. Reach out for guest pass details.

How does billing work?

Membership is billed monthly via card on file. Personal training and private pilates sessions are charged at the time of booking.

Are classes drop-in, or do I need to book?

Pilates classes are reserved through our member app. Booking opens several days in advance, and the membership add-on includes unlimited class reservations.

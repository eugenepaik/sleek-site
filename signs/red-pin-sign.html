Equipment Notice

Pull the red pin.
Leave the blue pin alone.

Red Pin
Pull to adjust
This is the weight-selector pin. Pull it out, choose your weight, and slide it back in.
Blue Pin
Do not pull
This pin holds the machine together. Removing it can damage the equipment and cause injury.
If the blue pin looks loose or out of place, do not use the machine — please let the front desk know so we can take a look. Thanks for helping us keep the floor in shape.
